About Me

Hello! My name is Zoey and welcome to my Portfolio. My journey as a web developer wasn't planned nor easy. It was quite a spare of the moment and came with some challenges. Upon graduating from Penn State University, I initially wanted to have a career in Healthcare. Although I was able to obtain opportunities in the healthcare industry, it wasn't fulfilling. These opportunities lead me to explore other career paths. My brother would always mention web development and coding and how I would be great at it. However, I wasn't interested until one random day at work, I decided to look up what web development is, and later that day, I enrolled in a Coding Boot Camp. To my readers, you may think this was a very rash and impulsive decision. However, it was the best decision I ever made in my life. After reading articles of women in tech and watching YouTube videos of different creative web applications, I knew this was the career path I want to take in life. After a long six months of learning web development, I am now a certified fullstack developer. Currently, I am pursuing a career in UX/UI Design and Front-End Development to show my creativity by planning, designing, developing, and implementing web and mobile applications. If you or your company need help in implementing beautiful and easy to navigate applications, I am your professional developer at your service!
